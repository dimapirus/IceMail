Cold emails for startups done right?

For years we were looking for a cold email outreach solution that just worked. The market is flooded with countless email marketing solutions, but let's be true to ourselves: for a startup company, they are just too demanding, complex and expensive.

We don't need to pay for overwhelming Salesforce integrations, complicated interfaces, sub-accounts and a lot of other enterprise level things. We just need something able to send cold and warm emails, track opens and take care of follow-ups. That doesn't need to cost us a used BWM per month.

That's why are building IceMail - a tool we were dreaming of for ourselves. Now our mission is to give thousands of other small businesses a modern, affordable and convenient solution for cold email campaigns.
